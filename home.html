Talking to AI:Prompt Engineering

Remember we used to fill words in our friend's mouth when our teachers suddenly asked questions between the lecture and we were not attentive in class? It was quick, clever and the only possible way to help them respond correctly without getting into trouble.This act is known as prompt.

Now that we’ve grown up, this simple habit has taken a more professional form. And this habit is called "prompt engineering". Then, we used to help our classmates to understand and answer the questions correctly and now,we are helping artificial intelligence systems, like chatbots or virtual assistants, to understand what we actually want.
